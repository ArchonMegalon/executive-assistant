Step 1

Choose the office shape this product will support.

Pick the option that matches the real daily rhythm. The right choice is about how commitments, approvals, and handoffs behave, not what the org chart says.

Personal

Best when one person wants a memo, a decision queue, and visible commitments across their own tools.

Shared workspace

Best when a small team shares the same incoming work, the same review queue, or the same follow-up list.

Executive support

Best when one executive needs heavier review, more context, and more explicit follow-up management around stakeholder work.
