What expands by tier

What buyers usually care about

Buyers want to know what changes as the workspace grows: operators, channels, review controls, and reporting. Keep those answers plain.

Typical plan differences
  • How many operators or reviewers share the workspace
  • How many channels are live and how much context they contribute
  • How strict the approval and review posture needs to be
  • How much admin visibility and control the team requires
Recommended buying path
  • Start where one operator can prove value from a real brief and draft queue
  • Add shared review and follow-up visibility when a team is clearing the same work
  • Move to the broadest tier when executive operations and admin controls become part of daily use
